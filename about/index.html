About us

HERMAN BROWN began life in 1983 as a creative collaboration between founder and owner Anna Nicholson and a chap with similar vintage interests (no, not Herman Brown himself). Initially, they built up the business via market stalls and fairs but soon, with a tiny budget and bucket loads of ideas, they opened up their first shop in Edinburgh. Business soon picked up a lively pace supported by regular overseas buying trips to Munich, Amsterdam and Seattle, as well as Brick Lane in London and the Barrowlands and Paddy's Market in Glasgow.
